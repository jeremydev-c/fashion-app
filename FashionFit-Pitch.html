Technology & Defensibility

Four layers.
Three never touch the API.

1
Item Understanding — GPT-4o Vision
Photo → structured JSON: category, color, formality, season, style axes. Runs once at upload. Zero cost at recommendation time.
2
Style DNA Engine — Pure Algorithm
Quiz answers + item uploads → weighted style vector. Updates on every upload. Zero AI cost. Compounds into a personalisation layer no competitor has.
3
Contextual Scoring — Pure JavaScript
Weather API + occasion + semantic scores → pre-filtered candidate shortlist. Zero AI cost. LLM receives only validated, ranked items.
4
AI Composition — GPT-4o-mini
Composes outfits from pre-filtered candidates with Style DNA in system prompt. One API call per request — on clean, structured input.

Why This Architecture Is Defensible

  • Cost scales sub-linearly: 3 of 4 layers have zero marginal AI cost. The LLM fires on clean, pre-validated data — not raw images or raw questions. COGS stays bounded as usage grows.
  • Style DNA as moat: every item uploaded, every outfit worn, every rating deepens the profile. A user with 6 months of history is exponentially harder to replicate on a competitor platform. The lock-in is structural.
  • Semantic intelligence: each wardrobe item carries formality, boldness, minimalism, warmth, and structure scores. These combine into signals no competitor is building — category gap detection, wear-frequency patterns, cross-wardrobe insights.
  • Compounding labelled dataset: at 100K users, Fashion Fit owns the largest real-wardrobe dataset in Africa. That dataset is training data for future models and a B2B data product. It cannot be replicated from scratch.
The Infrastructure Reality

Other apps show you clothes to buy. Fashion Fit builds a permanent record of what you own, understands how you dress, and gets more accurate over time. The longer you use it, the more it costs you to leave.
